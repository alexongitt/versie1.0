Hyein Seo

“The brand perfectly treads the line between masculine and feminine silhouettes with its oversized yet sexy styles”

Founded by the young South Korean designer’s name, Hyein Seo was launched in 2014 after Seo graduated from the Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp. While still in school, her bachelor collection was picked to be presented at the VFiles Made Fashion show during New York Fashion Week, and it wasn't long after that the British Fashion Council’s International Fashion Showcase for Emerging Talent awarded her with a Best Designer moniker.
